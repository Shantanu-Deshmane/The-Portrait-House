Frequently Asked Questions

circle How far in advance should I book?

We typically recommend booking 4-6 weeks in advance to ensure your preferred date, especially for weekend sessions.

circle Do you provide wardrobe styling?

Yes, every session includes a consultation where we discuss styling, and we have a curated studio wardrobe available for use.

circle What is your turnaround time for portraits?

The viewing appointment takes place 1 week after your session. Final prints and albums are typically ready within 3-4 weeks of selection.

circle Do you offer digital files?

We are a boutique studio specializing in high-quality physical prints, however, the matching digital file is included with every portrait purchased.
